Data-driven Astrophysics and Cosmology 
======

I am a PhD student in Simeon Bird's group at the University of California, Riverside. My research is in data-driven cosmology and astrophysics as enabled by numerical simulations and (astro)statistics. This means I'm somewhere between being an astronomer and a physicist, somewhere between a theorist, observer and statistician. 

I'm also a passionate advocate for improving the climate in physics and astronomy. I'm an ally and work to support a more diverse and inclusive community through the Physics Organization for Womxn and the Underrepresented (POWUR) at UCR. 

I talk about all of this on my blog.
