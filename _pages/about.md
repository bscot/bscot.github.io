I am a data oriented astrophysicist and cosmologist. I primarily work on current and next generation large surveys. By combining data from multiple surveys we can overcome limitations inherent to each, giving us a coherent picture of the evolution of the universe across cosmic time. Current focuses include,

- Subaru Observatory Prime Focus Spectrograph Galaxy Evolution Survey (PFS) 
- Cosmological Advanced Survey Telescope for Optical-UV Research (CASTOR)
- Vera C. Rubin Observatory Legacy Survey of Space & Time (LSST)
- Line Intensity Mapping Experiments

The goal of my work is to develop methods for combining these extremely large and heterogeneous datasets in order to enable reliable and principled astrophyical and cosmological inference.
