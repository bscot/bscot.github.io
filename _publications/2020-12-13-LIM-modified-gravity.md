This paper forecasts a measurement of deviations from General Relativity with CO Line Intensity Mapping. By considering a large and general family of modifications to General Relativity, we show that a CO line intensity mapping experiment should detect deviations from General Relativity with high statistical significance and provide a competitive and independent constraint to the Cosmic Microwave Background.
