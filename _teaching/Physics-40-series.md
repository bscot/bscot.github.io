Laboratory and discussion teaching assistant for mechanics for scientists and engineers. Topics in this calculus based sequence include Newton's laws applied to rotational and linear systems, conservation laws, and Newton's law of universal gravitation. Laboratory concepts focus on error analysis and developing an understanding of best practices in laboratory sciences. 

The focus of my approach to teaching in these courses is on developing a sense of professionalization, where students are encouraged to take responsibility for developing core skills that will be used in their major coursework while recieving practical and hands on support through small group activities.
