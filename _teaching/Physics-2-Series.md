Teaching Assistant for Laboratory and Discussion sections in Mechanics and Modern Physics for students in the life sciences. Topics in mechanics include vectors, Newton's laws, introductory linear and rotational dynamics, and Newton's law of universal gravitation. Topics in thermal physics include heat engines, the laws of thermodynamics, and the behavior of ideal gases, while E&M develops a familiarity with the notions of charge and applications to basic circuits. Topics in modern physics include vibrations, waves, optics, diffraction and refraction, the hydrogen atom and radiation physics and safety. 

In both discussion and laboratory settings, I focus on a hands on and collaborative based model of small group work, facilitated discussions, and self-reflection as a way to build student confidence and allow them to understand relationships between the course material both within a course and throughout the introductory series.
